# Landing Page Visual Spec (from screenshot)

This document describes the landing page exactly as seen in the provided screenshot. It is intended as a high-fidelity visual spec for recreating the layout, spacing, typography, colors, and components.

## Global System

### Layout & Grid
- **Format:** Mobile-first single column, centered content.
- **Max content width:** ~320–360px (content feels narrow with large side margins).
- **Horizontal padding:** ~20–28px.
- **Section spacing:** Large vertical rhythm, ~56–96px between major sections.
- **Alignment:** Primarily centered; card contents are left aligned where lists/forms appear.

### Color Palette (approximate)
- **Base background:** soft warm beige `#F7EEE6` (full-width).
- **Primary text on light:** deep brown `#3B2A1F`.
- **Secondary text on light:** warm gray-brown `#6E5A4C`.
- **Dark band background:** rich chocolate `#2E2018`.
- **Text on dark band:** warm cream `#F7EFE7`.
- **Accent / CTA:** muted coral `#D97862`.
- **Light tan accent:** `#E8C7A6`.
- **Card background:** white `#FFFFFF`.
- **Card shadow:** subtle, soft shadow `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` with large blur.

### Typography
- **Headlines:** modern serif (high contrast, elegant).
  - Sizes: ~28–36px in hero, ~22–28px in section headings.
  - Weight: medium/semibold.
- **Body text:** clean sans-serif.
  - Sizes: ~13–15px.
  - Line height: ~1.5–1.7.
- **CTA buttons:** sans-serif, 14–16px, medium weight.

### Spacing Scale (approximate)
- **XS:** 6–8px
- **S:** 12–16px
- **M:** 20–24px
- **L:** 32–40px
- **XL:** 56–72px

### Components
- **Buttons:** pill/rounded, solid coral fill, white text, medium padding (10–14px vertical, 18–24px horizontal).
- **Cards:** white, rounded corners (12–16px radius), soft shadow, internal padding ~20–24px.
- **Form fields:** stacked, light borders, rounded corners, small text.
- **Dividers:** small star/dot icons under headings, cream/gold tone.

## Section-by-Section

### 1) Hero
- **Background:** full-width beige.
- **Layout:** centered stack; large serif headline with a couple of words in coral.
- **Subcopy:** centered sans-serif paragraph beneath headline.
- **CTA:** small primary button (coral) below, centered.
- **Decor:** subtle circular/spot highlights in the background (very faint).

### 2) Primary Signup/Form Card
- **Background:** beige.
- **Card:** centered white card, rounded corners, soft shadow.
- **Content:** short heading and multiple stacked inputs (3–4 rows).
- **Spacing:** card separated from hero by a large vertical gap.

### 3) Dark Band Intro
- **Background:** full-width dark brown band.
- **Text:** centered cream headline (medium size).
- **Decor:** small star/dot icons below text (cream/gold).

### 4) Image + Text Cards (3 items)
- **Background:** beige.
- **Layout:** vertical stack of cards; each card is a horizontal split.
  - **Left:** rounded rectangular photo.
  - **Right:** title + short paragraph, left aligned.
- **Spacing:** generous gaps between cards.
- **Card style:** white, rounded, soft shadow.

### 5) Centered Statement
- **Background:** beige.
- **Content:** short centered serif headline with a small line of supporting text.
- **Minimal:** lots of whitespace.

### 6) Stats/Benefits Cards (3-up)
- **Background:** beige.
- **Layout:** three small white cards in a row (on narrow screens, likely stacked).
- **Each card:** bold number/title plus small supporting text.
- **Style:** rounded corners, soft shadow.

### 7) Dark Band Divider
- **Background:** dark brown band.
- **Content:** centered cream headline.
- **Decor:** small star/dot icons below.

### 8) Checklist/Form Card
- **Background:** beige.
- **Card:** centered white card with rounded corners.
- **Content:** small heading and a list/checklist-like rows (stacked).

### 9) Compact Signup Card
- **Background:** beige.
- **Card:** smaller white card, similar styling, fewer inputs.

### 10) Dark CTA Block
- **Background:** dark brown band.
- **Content:** centered cream headline + short paragraph.
- **CTA:** prominent coral rounded button, centered.

### 11) Testimonial / Quote
- **Background:** beige.
- **Content:** centered short paragraph (testimonial or reassurance).
- **CTA/Link:** small coral button or link below.

### 12) Final CTA / Footer
- **Background:** beige.
- **Content:** short centered line of text.
- **CTA:** coral rounded button, centered.
- **Footer microtext:** subtle small line beneath (lighter brown/gray).

## Component Inventory

### Buttons
- **Primary:** coral fill, white text, pill radius (20–999px).
- **Secondary/Small:** same style but smaller height.

### Cards
- **Standard card:** white, 12–16px radius, soft shadow.
- **Image+text card:** horizontal split with left image and right text.
- **Form card:** stacked inputs with subtle borders.

### Forms
- **Inputs:** stacked, light gray borders, rounded corners.
- **Spacing:** ~12–16px between fields.

### Decorative Icons
- **Stars/Dots:** small cream/gold icons beneath dark band headings.

### Imagery
- **Photos:** warm lifestyle imagery, rounded corners, subtle shadow if inside card.
